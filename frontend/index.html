About Us

Campus Vehicle is a student-built ride platform designed for university communities. It connects people who need a ride with verified drivers for two modes: Quick Drop for fast on-campus hops, and Route Share for customized long-distance rides where passengers share the cost of a journey.

Built with
Node.js · Express · MongoDB · Socket.io · JWT · Google Maps API
